Drop Us a Note

Corgi Hub is written for people who actually own Pembrokes, which means the people most likely to catch our mistakes are our readers. If something looks off — wrong information, a recommendation that doesn't match your experience, an important nuance we missed — write to us. We take it seriously.

Questions about your own corgi are also welcome, though we will always tell you when the right answer is "ask your vet" rather than invent an opinion. Corgi Hub does not give medical advice — we give information, and we know where that line is.

Where to Send It

The Corgi Hub Writing Desk is at: hello@pembroke-welsh-corgi-hub.github.io

Link the article if you're writing about something specific. If you're asking a general corgi question, just ask — we will route it to whoever is most likely to have a useful answer. The Writing Desk checks this regularly.

We don't reply to SEO outreach or collaboration pitches sent via this address.
