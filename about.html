About Pembroke Welsh Corgi Guide

Pembroke Welsh Corgi Guide is a practical resource focused on everyday ownership decisions, breed care, and simple routines that hold up in real life.

This site is intentionally focused on useful owner decisions rather than filler. We prioritize routines, comfort, daily care, and breed-specific trade-offs.

The first live publish on this site is a test article proving the content and deployment pipeline. From here, the plan is to expand into feeding, training, gear, health, and seasonal care topics.
